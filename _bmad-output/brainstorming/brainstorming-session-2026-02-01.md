### SCAMPER Method Overview

SCAMPER is a systematic creativity technique using 7 lenses to refine ideas:
- **S**ubstitute: What could you substitute?
- **C**ombine: What could you combine?
- **A**dapt: How could you adapt?
- **M**odify: What could you modify?
- **P**ut to other uses: New applications?
- **E**liminate: What could you remove?
- **R**everse: What if you reversed the approach?

- **Reverse: Materials find folders:** Instead of PMMs placing materials, could materials automatically find correct folders?
